# MA1508E - Linear Algebra for Engineering

## Overview
This note summarizes the key concepts and examination structure for the MA1508E course at the National University of Singapore. The course focuses on essential topics in [[Linear Algebra]], including vector spaces, eigenvalues, and matrix operations. The examination format is designed to assess students' understanding through a series of structured questions that require both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

## Key Concepts & Definitions
- **Matrix**: A rectangular array of numbers arranged in rows and columns.
- **Column Space**: The set of all possible linear combinations of the column vectors of a matrix.
- **Row Space**: The set of all possible linear combinations of the row vectors of a matrix.
- **Null Space**: The set of all vectors that, when multiplied by the matrix, yield the zero vector.
- **Eigenvector**: A non-zero vector that changes by only a scalar factor when a linear transformation is applied.
- **Determinant**: A scalar value that can be computed from the elements of a square matrix, providing important properties about the matrix.

## Detailed Technical Breakdown

### Examination Instructions
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Format**: Open book, proctored via Zoom
- **Materials Allowed**: MATLAB, scientific or graphical calculators
- **Submission**: Candidates must scan and submit their work in a specified format.

### Question Breakdown
1. **Question 1**: Focuses on the properties of a given matrix \( A \).
   - **(a)** Basis for the column space of \( A \).
   - **(b)** Basis for the row space of \( A \).
   - **(c)** Nullspace of \( A \).
   - **(d)** Verification of vectors in the column space.
   - **(e)** Projection of vectors onto the column space.
   - **(f)** Finding an orthogonal vector.
   - **(g)** Verification of a basis for the column space.
   - **(h)** Finding a specific transformation.

2. **Question 2**: Investigates conditions for full rank and properties of matrix \( A \).
   - **(a)** Conditions on \( a \) for full rank.
   - **(b)** Reduced row-echelon form and invertible matrix \( P \).
   - **(c)** Determinants of \( A A^T \) and \( A^T A \).
   - **(d)** Orthogonal diagonalization of \( R R^T \).

3. **Question 3**: Explores eigenvalues and differential equations.
   - **(a)** Verification of eigenvectors and fundamental solutions.
   - **(b)** Conditions for nontrivial solutions and general solutions of differential equations.
