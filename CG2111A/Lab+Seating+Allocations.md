# Lab Seating Allocations for CG2111A

## Overview
This note provides a structured overview of the seating allocations for the CG2111A module, detailing the pairwork and project groups assigned to each student. The allocations are categorized by lab sections B01 to B05, facilitating easy reference for both students and instructors. Understanding these allocations is crucial for effective collaboration and project management within the course.

## Key Concepts & Definitions
- **Pairwork Group**: A designated group for collaborative tasks among students.
- **Project Group**: A team assigned for project-based work, often involving more extensive collaboration.
- **Lab Sections**: Different physical or virtual spaces where students engage in practical learning activities.
