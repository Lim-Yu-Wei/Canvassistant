## How This Works

- **Automated pipeline** scrapes Canvas hourly for new PDFs
- New files are summarised (GPT-4o map-reduce) and saved as Obsidian notes
- Notes are committed to GitHub → `git pull` locally to sync
- Hand-crafted notes (like the ones here) coexist with auto-generated ones
