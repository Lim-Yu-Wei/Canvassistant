# Career Events for Semester 2 AY2526

## Overview
This note summarizes key career-related events scheduled for Semester 2 of AY2526, aimed at helping students prepare for internships and job opportunities. The events include briefings, seminars, and workshops designed to enhance communication skills and provide insights into industry landscapes. Students are encouraged to participate actively to maximize their career prospects.

## Key Concepts & Definitions
- **Internship**: A temporary position that offers practical experience in a professional setting.
- **Career Fest**: An event where students can interact with potential employers and learn about job opportunities.
- **Communication Skills**: The ability to convey information effectively and efficiently in various contexts.
