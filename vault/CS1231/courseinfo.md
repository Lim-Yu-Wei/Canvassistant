# CS1231: Discrete Structures Lecture Notes

## Overview
This note summarizes the key aspects of the CS1231 Discrete Structures course, including assessment details, attendance policies, and consultation information. The course emphasizes the importance of participation and provides opportunities for bonus marks through attendance and tutorial presentations. Students are encouraged to engage actively with the material to enhance their understanding and performance.

## Key Concepts & Definitions
- **Discrete Structures**: A branch of mathematics dealing with countable, distinct objects.
- **Assessment**: The evaluation of student performance through tests and exams.
- **Bonus Marks**: Additional points awarded for participation in lectures and tutorials.
- **Consultation**: Opportunities for students to seek help from the lecturer.
- **Attendance Policy**: Guidelines governing the recording and implications of attendance.

## Detailed Technical Breakdown

### Assessment Structure
- **Test 1**: 
  - Weight: 20%
  - Scope: Week 1 – Week 4 Lecture Notes and Tutorial 1 – Tutorial 2
  - Date: 11 Feb, Week 5, 12:00-13:00
- **Test 2**: 
  - Weight: 20%
  - Scope: Week 5 – Week 9 Lecture Notes and Tutorial 3 – Tutorial 7
  - Date: 25 Mar, Week 10, 12:00-13:00
- **Final Exam**: 
  - Weight: 60%
  - Scope: All Lecture Notes and Tutorials
  - Date: 4 May, 17:00-19:00

### Consultation Details
- **Slots**: Available in Week 4–5 and Week 9–10, announced on Canvas.
- **Other Consultations**: By appointment via email.

### Attendance Policy
- **Recording**: Attendance is recorded but not compulsory.
- **Bonus Marks**: Earned through attendance and participation.
- **Maximum Bonus**: 
  - 0 stars: Up to 2 bonus marks
  - 1 star: Up to 3 bonus marks
  - 2+ stars: Up to 4 bonus marks

### Makeup Test Policy
- **Valid Reasons for Absence**: Medical certificates, timetable clashes, competitions, etc.
- **Makeup Tests**: Offered for missed tests with valid reasons, typically more difficult than regular tests.

> [!important] **Note**: Attendance points and stars are updated on Canvas every 1-2 weeks.

> [!warning] **Important**: Students who miss the final makeup test will receive a score of ZERO for the corresponding tests.
