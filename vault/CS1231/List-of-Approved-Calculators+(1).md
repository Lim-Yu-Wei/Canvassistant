# List of Approved Calculators for CS1231

## Overview
This note outlines the approved calculators for the CS1231 module, adapted from the Singapore Examination and Assessment Board (SEAB) guidelines for the GCE A-Level examination. Candidates must adhere to specific restrictions regarding calculator functionalities, ensuring a fair examination environment. The list includes both scientific and graphing calculators that meet the criteria set forth by the examination board.

## Key Concepts & Definitions
- **Approved Calculators**: Calculators that meet specific criteria for use in examinations, excluding those with advanced functionalities such as symbolic algebra manipulations.
- **Scientific Calculators**: Devices designed for performing scientific calculations, typically including functions for trigonometry, logarithms, and statistical analysis.
- **Graphing Calculators**: Advanced calculators capable of plotting graphs and solving equations graphically.
